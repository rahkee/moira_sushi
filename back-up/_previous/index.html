About Moira

Japanese Influences + Global Inspirations

Our exciting menu expands on the Japanese tradition with bold new flavors and influences. We would like to explore the combinations of local seasonal ingredients with the infinite spectrum of elements around the globe. At Moira we pair traditional Japanese aesthetics with new refreshing flavors and textures to create a unique and vibrant dining experience with endless boundaries.
